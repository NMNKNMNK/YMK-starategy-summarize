🌸 軍拡ってなぁに？

軍拡は二週間ごとに繰り返している他サーバーとの対戦イベントです(*'∀')。GvG（Guild vs Guild）,Arms raceともいわれます。

月曜日から金曜日までの準備段階、土曜日の戦争段階に分かれています。準備段階に勝利したギルドが土曜日に相手サーバーに攻撃ができる。長い戦いです！

🌈 軍拡の重要性

① 負けた場合、対戦ギルドが越境してきて攻撃するが、勝てば対戦相手は越境してこないため、攻撃されることがない。

② サーバー内のギルド間では「対戦相手の越境を防ぐ」ことがギルドの価値の指標になりつつある
= 価値あるギルドとして遺跡の獲得などの交渉も有利になる可能性あり（嬉しい）

③ 日々のミッション(宝箱を開ける)をこなすと手に入りにくいURの欠片、専用装備万能欠片が手に入る（ハッピー）

🌟 まとめ

以上を踏まえて、軍拡でポイントを稼ぐことは個人の勝利、引いてはギルドのためになります👊

では、軍拡のミッションを出来るだけ楽にこなしつつ、ポイントを稼ぐにはどうしたらいいのか。
みんなの意見を聞きながら、まとめました！！
これであなたも軍拡マスター✨✨✨

🌸 What is Military Expansion?

Military Expansion is a battle event against other servers that repeats every two weeks (*'∀'). It's also called GvG (Guild vs Guild) or Arms race.

It's divided into preparation stages from Monday to Friday and the war stage on Saturday. Guilds that win the preparation stage can attack the opposing server on Saturday. It's a long battle!

🌈 Importance of Military Expansion

① If you lose, opposing guilds will cross over and attack, but if you win, opponents won't cross over, so you won't be attacked.

② Among guilds within the server, "preventing opponent crossover" is becoming a measure of guild value
= As a valuable guild, you may have advantages in negotiations such as relic acquisition (happy)

③ By completing daily missions (opening treasure chests), you can obtain hard-to-get UR fragments and universal equipment fragments (happy)

🌟 Summary

Based on the above, earning points in military expansion leads to individual victory and ultimately benefits the guild👊

So, how can we complete military expansion missions as easily as possible while earning points?
We've compiled this based on everyone's opinions!
Now you too can become a military expansion master✨✨✨

📅 1001 月・領地建造

1日目　領地建設

効率よくポイントを稼ぐには、事前の準備が大事です。
また、課金ですが建築数を増やす枠は購入をおすすめします。

得点源

  1. 素材（木材、石材、ルビー、巨竜の結晶）を使う♦️
    各使用する素材によって得られるポイントが異なります。
  2. 建造時間の短縮⏰
    普段の銀の宝箱やデーリータスクからの短縮アイテムを取得しておくことが鍵です。
    コツコツ溜めておきましょう。
  3. 施設戦力ポイント⚔️
    溜めておいた宝箱をタップすると得られます。

イベントコツ

  1. イベント数日前から施設を建設して受け取らずに置いておく🏘️
  2. 長い建設は事前に始めておき、当日完了するよう調整しておく🏚️
  3. 素材をたくさん使う建築をこの日に開始する👍
  4. 時間短縮はこの期間（月・火）に優先的に実施する⏰

※ (1)と(2)が効果的👌
※これに慣れると、次週行われるギルドコンテストと合わせ技にも使えます。

📅 1001 Monday・Territory Construction

Day 1　Territory Construction

Advance preparation is crucial for earning points efficiently.
Also, while it involves spending, we recommend purchasing additional construction slots.

Point Sources

  1. Use materials (wood, stone, ruby, giant dragon crystals)♦️
    Points earned vary depending on the materials used.
  2. Construction time reduction⏰
    The key is to obtain reduction items from regular silver chests and daily tasks.
    Accumulate them steadily.
  3. Facility power points⚔️
    Obtained by tapping accumulated treasure chests.

Event Tips

  1. Start building facilities several days before the event and leave them unclaimed🏘️
  2. Start long constructions in advance and adjust to complete on the day🏚️
  3. Start constructions that use lots of materials on this day👍
  4. Prioritize time reductions during this period (Mon・Tue)⏰

※ (1) and (2) are effective👌
※Once you get used to this, you can also use combination techniques with guild contests held the following week.

🔷 1002 Tuesday・Scientific Research

Day 2　Scientific Research

#ScientificResearch/MilitaryExpansion

Point Sources

  1. Use materials (wood, stone, ruby, giant dragon crystals)♦️
    Points earned vary depending on the materials used.
  2. Research time reduction⏰
    The key is to obtain reduction items from regular silver chests and daily tasks.
    Accumulate them steadily.
  3. Scientific power points🔬
    Obtained by tapping accumulated treasure chests.

Event Tips

  1. Start two long researches in advance and claim them at 11 AM reset time🤗
  2. Reduce time for researches started after reset time on the day⏰
    If possible, get cooperation counts from other members first to reduce time reduction items somewhat(・∀・)Nice!!
  3. If Radiant Research Institute is not unlocked → Do "Basic" and "Advanced".
    Early S2 research in the "Research Institute" gives few power-up points.
    It's good to progress "Basic" and "Advanced" research👌
  4. If Radiant Research Institute is unlocked. Prioritize radiant research.
    There are researches that increase each attribute hero level by up to 10.
    Aim for that first.
    Research Institute + Radiant Research Institute gives a total of maximum three slots🙌
    Do both (1) and (2) to efficiently get points!
  5. For material consumption, choose those using giant dragon crystals to earn points easily
  6. (1) is extremely important.
    Let's effectively use the total maximum three slots from Research Institute + Radiant Research Institute🙌

❄️ 氷原の戦場

💥 概要

基本的なマップは古戦場と変わりません。ただし、ポイントの稼ぎ方が大きく異なります💥

👊 基本的なポイントの取り方

とにかく、6分毎に出現する「魔法水晶鉱山」=「Mana mine」の占拠でポイントを稼ぐことが勝利の鍵です👊

この"魔法水晶鉱山"場所はマップの真ん中の両脇です。始まってすぐ出現します。動けるようになったらすぐに取りに行こう😙

そのあとは残り38分ごろから6分刻みで鉱山は出現するよ👍

鉱山を見つけたプレーヤーはギルドチャットにすぐに共有してね👍早ーくみつけてみんなで取りにいったギルドがとても有利🥳ここ、定期テストに出るから覚えてね！🥳

🤯 強い人の戦い方

残り時間が半分になると、マップの中心に「浮遊島」＝「Sky island」が出現します🤯

基本的にここは30m~40mのプレイヤーが集まるので強い人はこの浮遊島の占拠を目指してください👍

😎 中盤～後半までの戦い方

浮遊島が出現したあとは、浮遊島の四角に四つの施設が出現。ここに移動すると他の三つのサーバーと乱戦する可能性があるけど、鉱山にとても近くなるという大きなメリットがあるよ…😎もちろん、相手の拠点を攻撃して成功した場合も、点数に加算されるよ 😙 つまり、ここでの戦い方はあなたのテクニックしだいだね！

以上が氷原の戦場の大まかな戦い方

とにかく宝箱を見つけたら採取＆みんなに共有😎これが一番大事！ほかのギルドと戦いたい人は、浮遊島とその周辺の四つの施設で遊ぼう🥳

❄️ Frozen Battlefield

💥 Overview

The basic map is the same as the ancient battlefield. However, the way to earn points is significantly different💥

👊 Basic Point Earning Method

Anyway, occupying the "Mana mine" that appears every 6 minutes is the key to victory👊

The location of this "Mana mine" is on both sides of the middle of the map. It appears immediately after starting. Go get it as soon as you can move😙

After that, mines appear every 6 minutes starting from around 38 minutes remaining👍

Players who find mines should immediately share in guild chat👍Guilds that find quickly and go together are very advantageous🥳Remember this, it'll be on the regular test!🥳

🤯 How Strong Players Fight

When half the time remains, a "Sky island" appears in the center of the map🤯

Basically, 30m~40m players gather here, so strong people should aim to occupy this floating island👍

😎 Mid to Late Game Fighting

After the floating island appears, four facilities appear around it. Moving here may cause battles with other three servers, but there's a big advantage of being very close to mines…😎Of course, if you successfully attack enemy bases, points are also added 😙 So, how you fight here depends on your technique!

This is the general fighting strategy for the frozen battlefield

Anyway, if you find treasure chests, collect & share with everyone😎This is most important! Those who want to fight other guilds, let's play at the floating island and its surrounding four facilities🥳
