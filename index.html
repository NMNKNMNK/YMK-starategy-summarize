üå∏What is Guild Military Expansion?

Guild Military Expansion is a battle event against other servers that repeats every two weeks (*'‚àÄ'). Also known as GvG (Guild vs Guild) or Arms race.
It consists of preparation stages from Monday to Friday, and a war stage on Saturday. Guilds that win the preparation stage can attack the opponent server on Saturday. It's a long battle!

üåàImportance of Guild Military Expansion

‚ë† If you lose, the opposing guild will cross borders and attack, but if you win, the opponent won't cross borders, so you won't be attacked.

‚ë° Among guilds within the server, "preventing opponent border crossing" is becoming an indicator of guild value

‚ë¢ By completing daily missions (opening treasure chests), you can obtain hard-to-get UR fragments and universal equipment fragments (happy!)

The basic map is the same as the ancient battlefield. However, the way to earn points is significantly differentüí•

üëäBasic Point Earning Methods

After that, mines appear every 6 minutes starting from around 38 minutes remainingüëç
Players who find mines should immediately share in guild chatüëç Guilds that find quickly and go together have a huge advantageü•≥ Remember this, it'll be on the regular test!ü•≥

ü§ØStrong Player Strategy

When half the time remains, "Sky island" appears at the map centerü§Ø
Basically, 30m~40m players gather here, so strong players should aim to occupy this sky islandüëç

üòéMid to Late Game Strategy

After the sky island appears, four facilities appear around the sky island. Moving here may result in chaotic battles with the other three servers, but you get the big advantage of being very close to the mines...üòé Of course, successfully attacking enemy bases also adds to your points üòô So, the fighting method here depends on your technique!

That's the general strategy for the frozen battlefield
The most important thing is to find treasure chests, collect them & share with everyoneüòé Those who want to fight other guilds should play at the sky island and its surrounding four facilitiesü•≥
